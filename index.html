About: 

Mandy Xie does research in the areas of robotics, artificial intelligence and computer vision. She is keen on autonomous mobile robots, and has been working in this area since her undergraduate study. She is currently working on a Robot Painting project advised by Dr. Frank Dellaert. Their goal is to create a robotic painter which can paint like a human artist and reproduce Renaissance oil painting masterpieces.
Mandy did her master degree in Aerospace Engineering, and worked in the area of dynamics and control. Before she came to IC department, she worked in a startup company for more than two years as the team leader for Mapping and Localization group.
